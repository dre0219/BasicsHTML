Best Rappers Ever!

These are considered to be the greatest to have stepped on Earth.

Playboi Carti

Playboi Carti, whose real name is Jordan Terrell Carter, is considered a talented rapper and songwriter known for his unique flow and catchy hooks. He has a large and dedicated fanbase, and has released multiple successful albums and singles. Some people may find his music enjoyable because of the energy and emotion he puts into his performances, as well as the beats and production of the songs.

Drake

Drake is considered to be a very talented artist because of his ability to create hit songs that appeal to a wide audience, his skillful use of melody and rhythm in his music, and his ability to effectively convey emotions and tell stories through his lyrics. He is also known for his ability to seamlessly blend different music genres, such as hip-hop, R&B, and pop.

Juice Wrld

Juice Wrld, whose real name was Jarad Anthony Higgins, was known for his emotive and introspective lyrics, as well as his ability to blend elements of hip-hop, rock, and R&B in his music. He was also praised for his versatility as an artist, and his ability to switch between singing and rapping seamlessly. His music often explored themes of love, heartbreak, and addiction, which resonated with many listeners.
